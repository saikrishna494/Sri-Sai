About Us

Established in 2009, Sri Sai Electronics & Sound Solutions has been a trusted name in providing high-quality audio and electronic solutions for over 16 years.

Our Story

Founded by Mr. Ramesh Macherla, a sound engineering expert with over 20 years of experience, Sri Sai Electronics began as a small repair shop and has grown into a comprehensive electronics and sound solutions provider.

Today, we serve a wide range of clients from individual consumers to large corporate events, educational institutions, and religious establishments across the region.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of audio and electronics solutions to meet all your needs.

Sound System Installation

Professional installation of sound systems for homes, businesses, religious places, and events. We ensure optimal acoustic performance and ease of use.

Electronics Repair

Expert repair services for all types of electronic devices including amplifiers, mixers, speakers, and other audio equipment with quick turnaround time.

Event Sound Management

Complete sound solutions for events of all sizes including concerts, corporate events, weddings, and festivals with professional sound engineers.

Home Theater Setup

Custom home theater design and installation services to create the perfect entertainment experience in your home with the latest technology.

Maintenance Contracts

Regular maintenance services to keep your sound and electronic systems in optimal condition, preventing costly breakdowns and extending equipment life.

Consultation Services

Expert advice on selecting the right audio equipment and electronics for your specific needs, budget considerations, and future scalability.
